About Dr. K.N. Chaturvedi

Dr. Kameshwar Nath Chaturvedi is a former Secretary to the Government of India, Ministry of Law and Justice (Legislative Department), Chairperson of the Expert Group on Societies Registration Act, and an alumnus of distinction at Kurukshetra University.
